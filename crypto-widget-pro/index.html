Display Crypto Ticker Widgets, Coin Price Lists, Tables, Multi-Currency Tabs, and Price Labels Anywhere on Your Website with Cryptocurrency Widgets Pro Shortcodes.

Cryptocurrency Widgets Pro is a WordPress plugin designed to display real-time cryptocurrency data on websites. It offers features such as current coin prices, price tickers, price tickersmarket cap, trading volume, and percentage changes for over 2,500 cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Dogecoin, and Solana. The plugin also provides the option to add any custom coins.The key feature of this plugin is the ability to show real-time updates. This plugin utilizes third-party crypto APIs to fetch the latest market prices of virtual coins by quickly retrieving data from leading crypto exchanges.

Key Features

  • Live Price Ticker: Display real-time cryptocurrency prices in a scrolling ticker. Place it in your website’s header, footer, or anywhere on your site.
  • Multi-Fiat Currency Support: Convert and display cryptocurrency prices in over 15 different fiat currencies, like USD, EUR, and GBP, for a convenient global experience.
  • Real-Time Binance Data: Utilize Binance’s live data API for accurate updates on supported cryptocurrencies, ensuring current market info.
  • 24-Hour Percentage Changes: Show cryptocurrency price changes over the last 24 hours, allowing users to see quick market trends.
  • Multiple Widget Styles: Choose from various widget types, such as price tickers, coin lists, and charts, to customize the layout as per your needs.
  • Advanced Tables and Multi-Currency Tabs: Create detailed tables of multiple cryptocurrency prices and add tabs so users can quickly switch between different currencies or exchanges.
  • Crypto News Feeds: Display real-time cryptocurrency news directly on your website to keep visitors informed on the latest updates.
  • Crypto Calculator: This feature enables visitors to convert any crypto coin price in 30+ fiat currencies or other cryptocurrencies.
  • Historical Chart: Show a one-year historical price chart for any cryptocurrency using the Chart Widget. Choose default options or integrate with third-party services like CryptoCompare and TradingView.
  • Donation Box Widget Easily add a crypto donation widget to your site with multiple styles, like lists or tables. You can also add various wallet options such as Bitcoin and Metamask, providing flexibility for donations.
  • Price Block Widget Easily embed live cryptocurrency price blocks directly within your blog content. Keep your readers informed with real-time price updates of cryptocurrencies.
  • Price Card Widget This feature adds real-time cryptocurrency price cards to your website or blog, allowing your readers to stay updated with live data.
  • Price Slider Widget Add a stylish, sliding display of crypto coin prices at the top or bottom of your content, providing readers with real-time updates on the latest prices.
  • CoinGecko Widgets Easily embed CoinGecko’s crypto widgets on your WordPress site with a simple shortcode, displaying live crypto prices and updates directly within your content.
  • Custom CSS: Customize widget appearances with custom CSS to match your website's look.
  • Multi-Currency & Language Support: Display prices in any currency. The plugin supports multiple languages, making your site accessible to a global audience.
  • Compatibility with WordPress Themes: This plugin is compatible with all WordPress themes.
  • Fully Responsive: Ensures your site looks visually appealing across all devices.
  • Premium Support: As a Pro user, receive dedicated support from the Cool Plugins premium support team within 24 hours.
